Thiruvarur, India

Location

Thiruvarur is located 61 kms east of Thanjavur in Tamilnadu.

About

Weather

The wet season is overcast, the dry season is windy and mostly cloudy, and it is hot and oppressive year round.

Population

Out of total population of Tiruvarur, 1,403,348 in the district, 257,795 are in urban area and 1,006,482 are in rural area. 65,220 households are in urban, 261,999 are in rural area.

Sports

Cricket and Kabadi are popular sports in Thiruvarur.

Things to do

  • Chariot festival
  • Muthupet Lagoon
  • Udayamarthandapuram Bird Sanctuary
